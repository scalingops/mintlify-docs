## Recently Shipped

### MSP Tools

A suite of tools purpose-built for MSP operations, giving you clear visibility into client health, financial performance, and tool stack coverage. [Read the full documentation](/user-guides/msp-tools/index).

**Financial Reporting** - Clear visibility into the profitability of every client relationship.
- **Managed Revenue** - Recurring revenue from managed service contracts
- **Support Hours** - Time spent on reactive support from configured service boards
- **Managed Endpoints** - Device or user counts based on your chosen endpoint model
- **Reactive Hours per Endpoint (RHEM)** - See how much reactive work each endpoint generates
- **Effective Hourly Rate (EHR)** - Know what you're actually earning per hour of support
- **All-in Seat Price** - Your true per-seat economics
- **Financial Grading** - A-F letter grades so you can instantly spot your most and least profitable accounts
- **Flexible Reporting Periods** - Current month, trailing 3 months, year-to-date, or any specific historical month

**Client Health** - A comprehensive health and success platform that tells you exactly where to focus your resources.
- **Health Scoring Across 8 Dimensions** - Service Quality, Engagement, Financial Health, Sales Pipeline, Projects, Tool Stack, Environment, and Operational
- **Risk Classification** - Every company categorized as Thriving, Healthy, At Risk, or Critical with trend indicators
- **Portfolio Summary** - Your entire book of business at a glance with risk distribution, NPS trends, and engagement gaps

**Tool Stack** - Understand tool deployment across your entire client base.
- Track deployment percentage of required and recommended tools per company
- Identify gaps where clients are missing critical tools
- Automatic planner item creation for deployment tracking
- Drive standardization and upsell opportunities across your managed environment

## In Development

### New Integrations

- **Acronis Integration** - Sync backup and disaster recovery data from Acronis Cyber Protect Cloud
- **Liongard Integration** - Pull configuration data from Liongard with automatic compliance checks that continuously validate your environments against expected configurations

### Hudu & IT Glue Integrations

Deep integrations with Hudu and IT Glue so your clients never need another portal — and you don't have to pay for MyGlue.

- **Articles** - Sync and view knowledge base articles directly within MSPortal with full RBAC control
- **Flexible Assets** - Access flexible asset data from your documentation platform, scoped by company and role permissions
- **Passwords** - Securely surface passwords to authorized users without leaving MSPortal, governed by role-based access controls
- **Procedures** - View standard operating procedures and runbooks alongside the rest of your client data

All synced data respects MSPortal's RBAC system, ensuring users only see what their role permits. Everything your clients need in one place — no separate documentation portal required.

## Pending Approval

### Chrome Extension

Our Chrome extension is complete and currently awaiting Google approval. Once approved, you'll be able to access MSPortal directly from your browser without leaving your current tab.

**Features:**
- **Tickets View**: See your assigned tickets with priority and status indicators
- **Training View**: Continue training courses directly from the extension
- **AI Chat**: Ask questions about clients, tickets, or compliance data
- **Secure Authentication**: Sign in once with your existing MSPortal credentials
