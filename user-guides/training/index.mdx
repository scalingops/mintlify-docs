The Training module in MSPortal.ai provides a complete learning management system to help your organization deliver, track, and manage employee training programs.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/training/training-overview.png" alt="Training Module Overview" />
</Frame>

## Key Features

- **Course Library** - Access a catalog of pre-built courses or custom training content
- **Progress Tracking** - Monitor individual and team training completion rates
- **Learning Paths** - Create structured training programs with multiple courses
- **Certifications** - Track compliance certifications and renewal dates
- **Reporting** - Generate detailed training reports for compliance and auditing

## Accessing the Training Module

Navigate to **Training** from the main sidebar navigation. The Training Menu provides quick access to all training features:

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/training/training-menu-dropdown.png" alt="Training Menu" />
</Frame>

### Menu Options

| Option | Description | Permission Required |
|--------|-------------|---------------------|
| Course Library | Browse and enroll in available courses | All users |
| My Training | View your enrolled courses and progress | All users |
| All Enrollments | View all user enrollments across organization | Manage Training |
| Learning Paths | Create and manage learning paths | Write Training |
| Reports & Analytics | Access training reports and metrics | Write Training |

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse Course Library">
    Navigate to **Training > Course Library** to see all available courses. You can filter by category, search by name, or switch between grid and list views.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enroll in a Course">
    Click on any course card to view details, then click **Enroll** to add it to your training. Enrolled courses appear in **My Training**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Training">
    Open an enrolled course and work through the content. Your progress is automatically tracked and saved.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Tasks

### Enrolling in a Course

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a Course">
    Browse the Course Library or use the search bar to find specific training topics.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Course Details">
    Click on a course card to see the description, duration, and any prerequisites.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Enroll">
    Click the **Enroll** button to add the course to your training queue.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
You can also be enrolled in courses automatically through Learning Paths assigned by your administrator.
</Tip>

### Taking a Course

Once enrolled, courses can be accessed from **My Training**. The course player supports multiple content types:

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/training/course-player.png" alt="Course Player" />
</Frame>

**Supported Content Types:**
- **SCORM Packages** - Interactive e-learning content with progress tracking
- **Video Courses** - Video-based training with completion tracking
- **Documents** - PDF and document-based training materials

**Player Features:**
- Progress bar showing completion percentage
- Session time tracking
- Fullscreen mode for focused learning
- Automatic progress saving

### Working with Learning Paths

Learning Paths allow administrators to create structured training programs by grouping multiple courses together.
