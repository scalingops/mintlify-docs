Billing Settings allows you to manage your MSPortal.ai subscription, payment methods, add-on services, and view billing history. Only Primary Admins can access billing settings.

## Accessing Billing Settings

1. Click **Settings** in the sidebar
2. Select the **Billing** icon from the settings navigation

## Subscription Management

### Current Plan

View your active subscription details:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Plan Name** | Your current subscription tier |
| **Status** | Active, Trial, or Expired |
| **Billing Cycle** | Monthly or Annual |
| **Next Billing Date** | When your next payment is due |

### Choosing a Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Choose a Plan">
    Select the plan selection button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compare Plans">
    Review features and pricing for each tier
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Plan">
    Choose the plan that fits your needs
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Enter payment details to activate
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Payment Methods

### Setting Up Billing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Set Up Billing">
    Open the payment configuration
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Card Details">
    Provide credit card or payment information
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Address">
    Confirm billing address
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Payment Method">
    Complete the setup
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Managing Payment Methods

- **Update card** - Change to a new payment method
- **Set default** - Choose primary payment method
- **Remove** - Delete saved payment methods

## Billing History

### Viewing Invoices

Access past invoices and receipts:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Date** | Invoice date |
| **Amount** | Total charged |
| **Status** | Paid, Pending, or Failed |
| **Download** | PDF invoice download |

### View All Invoices

Click **View all invoices** to see complete billing history with options to:
- Download individual invoices
- Filter by date range
- Export billing reports

## Billing FAQs

### When am I charged?
Charges occur at the start of each billing cycle (monthly or annually).

### Can I change my plan?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

### How do I cancel?
Contact support to cancel your subscription. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.

### What payment methods are accepted?
MSPortal accepts major credit cards through Stripe, including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.
