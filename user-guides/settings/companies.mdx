Companies Settings allows you to manage all client companies in your MSPortal.ai instance. Add new companies, configure their settings, and manage company-specific configurations.

## Accessing Companies Settings

1. Click **Settings** in the sidebar
2. Select the **Companies** icon from the settings navigation

## Companies List

The Companies page displays all client companies in your organization.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/settings/settings-companies-list.png" alt="Companies List" />
</Frame>

### Company Information

Each company entry shows:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Name** | Company name |
| **Industry** | Business sector |
| **Primary Contact** | Main point of contact |
| **Status** | Active or inactive |
| **Users** | Number of associated users |

### Adding a New Company

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Add Company">
    Select the **+ Add Company** button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Company Details">
    Fill in company name, industry, and contact information
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Set company-specific configurations like timezone and preferences
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Contacts">
    Add primary and secondary contacts for the company
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to create the company
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Company Details

Click on any company to view and edit its details:

### Basic Information
- **Company Name** - Legal or display name
- **Industry** - Business sector classification
- **Website** - Company website URL
- **Phone** - Main contact number

### Address Information
- **Street Address** - Physical location
- **City, State, Postal Code** - Full address details
- **Country** - Country of operation

### Company Preferences
- **Timezone** - Default timezone for the company
- **Language** - Preferred language for communications
- **Fiscal Year Start** - Financial year beginning

## Contacts Management

Each company can have multiple contacts:

| Contact Type | Description |
|--------------|-------------|
| **Primary Contact** | Main point of contact for the company |
| **Technical Contact** | IT or technical representative |
| **Billing Contact** | Finance or billing department |
| **Executive Contact** | C-level or decision makers |

### Adding a Contact

1. Navigate to the company's detail page
2. Click **Add Contact** in the Contacts section
3. Enter contact details (name, email, phone, title)
4. Select the contact type
5. Save the contact

## Integration with PSA

Companies sync with your connected PSA system:

- **Halo PSA** - Syncs as clients
- **ConnectWise** - Maps to company records
- **Autotask** - Syncs with accounts
- **Syncro** - Integrates with customers

<Note>
Changes made in your PSA will sync to MSPortal. Some fields may be read-only if managed by your PSA.
</Note>

## Company Assignments

When using company restrictions (configured in Tenant Settings), users must be assigned to companies to access their data.

### Assigning Users to a Company

1. Open the company details
2. Navigate to the **Assigned Users** section
3. Click **Add User**
4. Select users who should have access
5. Save changes

## Best Practices

- **Keep information current** - Regularly update company details
- **Verify contacts** - Ensure contact information is accurate
- **Use consistent naming** - Establish naming conventions for companies
- **Document relationships** - Track parent/child company relationships
