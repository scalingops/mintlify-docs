The Training Setup step configures MSPortal's training module, which lets you deliver security awareness training, onboarding courses, and other educational content to your clients' users.

## What You'll Configure

- **Training Courses** - Select or create courses for your clients
- **Enrollment Settings** - How users are enrolled in training
- **Completion Requirements** - Passing scores and deadlines
- **Notifications** - Reminders and completion alerts

## Steps to Complete

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Training Settings">
    Click **Go to Settings** in the onboarding wizard, or go to **Settings > Training**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Available Courses">
    Browse the built-in training courses. These cover common topics like security awareness, phishing prevention, and password hygiene.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Course Settings">
    For each course you want to use, configure:
    - Passing score requirements
    - Time limits (if any)
    - Certificate options
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Enrollment Rules">
    Decide how users get enrolled in training:
    - Automatic enrollment for new users
    - Manual enrollment by administrators
    - Scheduled enrollment campaigns
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Notifications">
    Set up reminder emails for incomplete training and notifications for completed courses.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Training Use Cases

- **Security Awareness** - Annual security training for all users
- **New Hire Onboarding** - Orientation training for new employees
- **Compliance Training** - HIPAA, PCI, or industry-specific requirements
- **Phishing Awareness** - Training on identifying phishing attempts

## Manual Completion

This step requires **manual completion**. Click **Mark Complete** in the wizard when you've finished setting up training.

<Tip>
  You can enable training for specific companies later. During onboarding, focus on understanding the available courses and basic settings.
</Tip>
