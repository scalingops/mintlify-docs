The Tenant Details step configures the foundational information about your MSP. This information appears on invoices, reports, and throughout the portal.

## What You'll Configure

- **Company Name** - Your MSP's business name
- **Business Address** - Street address, city, state/province, and postal code
- **Timezone** - Your primary operating timezone
- **Billing Email** - Where billing notifications are sent

## Steps to Complete

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Tenant Settings">
    Click **Go to Settings** in the onboarding wizard, or go to **Settings > Tenant** from the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Company Information">
    Fill in your company name and billing email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your Address">
    Enter your complete business address. Start typing to use the address autocomplete feature.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Your Timezone">
    Select your timezone from the dropdown. This affects how dates and times are displayed throughout the portal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Save Changes** at the bottom of the form.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Auto-Completion

This step is **automatically marked as complete** when you have:
- Entered an address (at minimum: street address and city)
- Selected a timezone

<Note>
  The address you enter here will be used on invoices and client-facing reports. Make sure it's your official business address.
</Note>
