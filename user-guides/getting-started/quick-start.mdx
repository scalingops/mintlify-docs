## Welcome to MSPortal.ai

MSPortal.ai is a comprehensive platform designed for Managed Service Providers to manage client relationships, track compliance, plan IT roadmaps, and deliver exceptional service. This guide will help you get oriented and productive quickly.

## Initial Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign In">
    Navigate to your organization's MSPortal.ai URL and sign in with your credentials. First-time users will receive an invitation email with login instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Company">
    Use the company selector in the top navigation bar to choose which client company you want to work with. The selected company determines what data you see throughout the platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore the Sidebar">
    The collapsible sidebar on the left provides access to all modules. Click the chevron icon to expand or collapse it, or use the keyboard shortcut `Ctrl+B` (Windows) or `Cmd+B` (Mac).
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Sidebar Structure

The sidebar is organized into three main categories:

**Planning**
- **Company Overview** - Dashboard with company health metrics, compliance status, and upcoming items
- **Surveys** - Client satisfaction surveys and feedback collection
- **Goals** - Strategic objectives and action items derived from surveys and planning
- **Planner** - Timeline view of IT roadmap items, projects, and device refresh cycles
- **Budgets** - Financial planning and budget tracking for client projects

**Operations**
- **Calendar** - Meeting scheduling, QBR planning, and calendar management
- **Microsoft 365** - M365 security scores and compliance (requires M365 integration)
- **Tickets** - Service desk tickets and requests (requires PSA integration)
- **Compliance** - Compliance frameworks, policies, and tracking
- **Training** - Security awareness training and course management
- **Devices** - Asset management and device inventory
- **External Resources** - Links to external tools and resources
- **Reporting** - Reports and analytics generation

**Admin**
- **Admin** - System administration portal (admin users only)
- **Settings** - User preferences, integrations, and configuration

<Tip>
Modules requiring integrations (Microsoft 365, Tickets) only appear after the relevant integrations are configured.
</Tip>

## Your First Tasks

### 1. Review the Company Overview

Start by visiting **Company Overview** to get a snapshot of:
- Compliance status and scores
- Recent activity and issues
- Upcoming planner items
- Key health metrics

### 2. Explore the Planner

Navigate to **Planner** to see the IT roadmap:
- Upcoming projects and tasks
- Device refresh schedules
- Milestone tracking
- Timeline visualization

Use the view controls to switch between Monthly, Quarterly, and Yearly views. You can also adjust the display density for more or less detail.

### 3. Configure Settings

Click **Settings** in the sidebar to access the settings drawer where you can:
- Update your user profile and avatar
- Configure notification preferences
- Set up integrations (if you have admin access)
- Customize your experience

## Key Features

### Company Context

<Warning>
Always verify you have the correct company selected before making changes. The company selector in the top bar determines which client's data you're viewing and editing.
</Warning>

### Global vs Company View

Many modules offer a "Global" view toggle that lets you see data across all companies at once. This is useful for:
- Identifying trends across your client base
- Finding items that need attention
- Generating cross-company reports

### Filtering and Search

Most modules include powerful filtering capabilities:
- Click the filter icon to access filter options
- Use search bars for quick text searches
- Filters persist within your session

### AI-Powered Features

<Info>
Look for AI-powered features throughout the platform, including intelligent recommendations in the Planner and Compliance modules.
</Info>

## Common Workflows

### For Account Managers

1. **Client Preparation**
   - Review Company Overview before client meetings
   - Check survey results and satisfaction scores
   - Review goals and their progress

2. **QBR Planning**
   - Use Calendar to schedule quarterly business reviews
   - Generate reports for meeting presentations
   - Track action items in Goals

### For Technical Staff

1. **Daily Operations**
   - Check assigned planner items
   - Review compliance status and alerts
   - Monitor device inventory

2. **Client Work**
   - Access client-specific compliance requirements
   - Generate technical reports
   - Update device information

### For Administrators

1. **Platform Setup**
   - Configure user accounts and permissions
   - Set up PSA and M365 integrations
   - Manage company settings and branding

2. **Ongoing Management**
   - Monitor integration health
   - Review user activity
   - Manage training assignments
