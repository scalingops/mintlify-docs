This guide walks you through connecting your Halo PSA instance to MSPortal.ai.

## Prerequisites

- Administrator access to your Halo PSA instance
- Access to MSPortal.ai with integration management permissions

## Setup Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Halo PSA">
    Navigate to your Halo PSA instance and log in with an administrator account.

    ![Halo PSA Login](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-12-08/5fff56a0-9e96-40e0-994a-2b9a404de3b7/stack_animation.webp)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    Click **Integrations** in the navigation menu.

    ![Click Integrations](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-12-08/a3ec20a0-304a-43e1-9a06-765959a91331/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=0,339&br_px=1548,1205&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=85,494)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create API Application">
    1. Click **View Applications**
    2. Create a new application named "MSPortal.ai"
    3. Select **Client ID and Secret (Services)** as the authentication method
    4. Save your **Client ID** and **Client Secret** - you'll need these later
    5. Select your **MSPortal** API-only agent (see [Create Custom Halo PSA Role and API User](/user-guides/integrations/Halo%20PSA%20Custom%20Role%20and%20User) to set this up)

    ![View Applications](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-12-08/40c555cd-f58c-47f3-b369-58a1a9940717/stack_animation.webp)

    <Warning>
      Make sure to save your Client ID and Client Secret immediately. The secret cannot be retrieved after creation.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure CORS Whitelist">
    1. Click **Security**
    2. Add `app.msportal.ai` to the CORS whitelist
    3. Click **Submit** to save

    ![Security Settings](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-12-08/8e3aefc3-50bd-4967-b2b3-56b00baad2dd/stack_animation.webp)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect in MSPortal.ai">
    1. In MSPortal.ai, navigate to **Settings** â†’ **Integrations**
    2. Click the **Halo PSA** integration card
    3. Enter your **Client ID** and **Client Secret**
    4. Enter your Halo PSA instance URL

    ![MSPortal Integration Settings](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-12-08/7cc63143-2d64-4c93-b193-7e609eb74d58/stack_animation.webp)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test the Connection">
    Click **Test Connection** to verify everything is configured correctly.

    ![Test Connection](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-12-08/6ce82c62-bd0d-41bc-9589-2f8538a5199e/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=187,243&br_px=1906,1205&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=532,343)

    You'll see a success notification in the top-right corner when the connection is successful.

    ![Success Notification](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-12-08/0ab9eee3-b317-40f1-bb7b-803ba98b0c73/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=187,0&br_px=1906,961&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=955,16)

    Click **Save** to save your credentials.

    ![Save Settings](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-12-08/f7517b63-46a3-44b6-b121-5b3e188c34e9/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=0,0&br_px=1719,961&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=471,250)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Sync Settings">
    Navigate to the **Sync Settings** tab to configure:

    - **Queues**: Select which ticket queues to sync with MSPortal.ai
    - **Status Rules**: Configure how ticket statuses map between systems
    - **Resolution Statuses**: Select statuses to link to the Resolution Status Stepper on the ticket menu

    ![Sync Settings](https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-12-08/2ce2d3b8-4825-4ac1-947f-f0205b92bc24/stack_animation.webp)

    <Tip>
      If you've made large-scale changes, click **Clear and Resync** to delete all synced tickets from MSPortal.ai and perform a fresh sync.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Opportunity Settings">
    Navigate to the **Opportunity Settings** tab to configure how sales opportunities sync between Halo PSA and MSPortal.ai.

    ![Opportunity Settings](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-12-08/8c38e5d0-394e-4668-9f8e-9ca85fd5d84a/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=0,0&br_px=1719,961&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=431,255)

    Click **Save Settings** when complete.

    ![Save Opportunity Settings](https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/https://colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2025-12-08/34b5aa8b-4e7b-4804-9bec-8805999fb3de/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=187,243&br_px=1906,1205&force_format=jpeg&q=100&width=1120.0&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=599,327)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Next Steps

Once your integration is configured, MSPortal.ai will begin syncing data from Halo PSA. You can:

- View synced tickets in the **Tickets** module
- Access customer data in the **Companies** module
- Create opportunities from the **Planner** module
- Link projects to planner items
